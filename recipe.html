Red Velvet Cheesecake Brownies

Ingredients

For Red Velvet Brownies

  1. 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  2. 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 1 1/2 teaspoons red food coloring
  5. 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  6. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  8. 3 tablespoons baking cocoa

For Cream Cheese Swirl

  1. 8 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  2. 1 egg
  3. 1/3 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F and line an 8 x 8 pan on all sides with parchment paper. Set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the melted butter and sugar. Whisk until glossy.
  3. Add the eggs and whisk until it emulsifies and is slightly foamy, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and food coloring and stir to evenly distribute.
  5. Add the flour, cocoa powder and salt and mix until combined and no streaks of flour remain.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan, spreading into an even layer.
  7. Next, make the cream cheese swirl. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and whisk together until smooth and creamy.
  8. Drop the cream cheese mixture in large dollops on top of the red velvet brownie batter. Use a butter knife to make big swirls.
  9. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a knife inserted into the middle comes out clean.
  10. Slice and Enjoy!
